Bio

Nivedita Bhadra, PhD, is an accomplished Data Scientist with over five years of industry experience following her PhD in Computational Physics from the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER), Kolkata. She specializes in applying advanced statistical analysis and machine learning techniques to derive actionable insights from complex datasets. Proficient in Python and R, Nivedita excels in statistical methods such as generalized linear models, multivariate regression, and time-series analysis. Her machine learning expertise includes neural networks, support vector machines, and random forests. She is skilled in SQL for data management and integration, and adept at data visualization using ggplot2, Matplotlib, and Seaborn. During her tenure at the Translational Genomic Research Institute (TGen), Nivedita developed predictive models, conducted multivariate analyses, and utilized AI to predict clinical outcomes. She has a proven track record of collaborating with interdisciplinary teams to support scientific research and present findings effectively. Nivedita is known for her excellent communication skills, project management capabilities, and the ability to work seamlessly within diverse teams. She is passionate about leveraging her data science expertise to drive innovative solutions and impactful projects in the industry.
